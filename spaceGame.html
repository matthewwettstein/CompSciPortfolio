NEON LASER TAG

Objective

The goal of the game is to survive in the deep space arena by tagging enemies and avoiding their laser fire. You must manage your health while clearing out waves of enemies to reach the highest wave possible.

Rules

  • Do not let your energy bar reach 0%.
  • Tagging enemies has a chance to spawn a energy pack.
  • Make sure to pick the best upgrades possible.

Scoring

After you take out every enemy, you move onto the next wave

Instructions

  • Use WASD to move
  • G to throw shock discs
  • Left mouse to shoot
  • Z for a secret...
